Bulma

A Brilliant Scientist

Bulma is the most significant female character from the popular anime/manga series, Dragon Ball.
She's a brilliant scientist and is the second daughter of Capsule Corporation's founder, Dr.Brief and his wife Panchy. She has an older sister named Tights.
She's the first friend of Goku, and is the ex-girlfriend of Yamcha, and later the wife of Vegeta. She has a son named Trunks and a daughter named Bulla.
While she cannot fight well, her gadgetry helped winning several battles and in the search for Dragon Balls.
